ABOUT US

MY TEQ Solutions is a software development & training company located in Tiruchirappalli, Tamil Nadu, India. It was started in the year of 2017 and incorporated under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Govt. of India as a corporate training and development company in 2019. It is internationally accredited as an ISO 9001:2015 certified training company by System and Product Certification, UK.


MY TEQ Software Solutions was started with the aim to train, develop and to give career guidance to students. It raises prominent, well-resourced employees to IT and ITES Industries. It offers end-to-end training programmes in programming, soft skills and interview training along with various collaborative software projects and real time scenarios for corporates and educational institutions.


MY TEQ Software Solutions was provides unique & friendly training with the latest technology required in IT Industries and 24/7 support, so that students can get knowledge and support anytime. It has experienced Subject Matter Experts (SME) in each domain and placed many students from various colleges. It conducts many value added courses, workshops and guest lectures in all the trending topics sporadically.
